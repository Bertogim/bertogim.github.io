Video Tools

These video tools are extremely useful, as they allow you to perform various processing tasks without the need to upload files to the cloud.
All processing is done directly on your computer, ensuring greater security and privacy.

Additionally, they are very easy to use, with simple interfaces that let you perform video compressions and other adjustments quickly and efficiently, without hassle.



1. Video Compressor

A useful video compression tool.
This application allows you to upload videos and compress them to the MB you want.

Example:

Original size: 91.84 MB

➡️

Compressed size: 8.56 MB

Video credits to daviddrm52

How Does It Work?

The tool uses FFmpeg, a video processing library, to perform compression.
You can adjust the quality and compression speed according to your preferences.
